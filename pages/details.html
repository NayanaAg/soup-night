Not sure what soup to bring?

Worry not! I have prepared an extremely easy "quiz" for you to decide on a soup you can bring. Choose the image below that speaks to you the most, and I'll recommend you a soup based on its vibes.

Crunchy autumn leaves

Tomato soup

As someone who enjoys warm and comforting flavours, tomato soup is the perfect choice for you! Its simple but delicious flavours make it one of the most popular classic soups in the world. Great choice!

An elegant study

French onion soup

You enjoy the finer things in life, and this is true even for your soup choices. The complex, old-fashioned flavour of french onion soup suits you very well. Fun fact, this is also Wally's favourite soup, so you may end up competing with him to see who can make the frenchest and onionest soup.

An exciting roller coaster

Tom yum soup

You are someone who enjoys the adrenalin rush of extreme sports (like eating food spicy enough to make you cry), and the strong hot and sour flavours of tom yum soup feel exactly like being on a rollercoaster. This is my favourite soup so you've objectively picked the best option of the six, congratulations!

Christmas with the family

Minestrone

You're someone who loves spending time with people, and the comforting, family-style Italian minestrone soup fits that perfectly. It's a soup that combines many different ingredients and flavours; the results could easily turn out chaotic, but somehow it's always warm and delicious!

A peaceful nature walk

Spinach soup

Some would say spinach soup is an unconventional choice, but I actually really enjoyed it when I tried it recently! It had a light, fresh, and herbal flavour, which reminds me of being on a relaxing nature trail through the woods. You're probably someone who enjoys spending quiet time in nature more than doing hectic city activties.

Sunrise by the beach

Egg drop soup

This picture is probably the least literal of the bunch -- something about the calm ocean with a sunrise just reminds me of an eggyolk gently dropping into a bowl of soup lol. The serene ocean matches the gentle taste of this soup -- it may not be the most exciting soup, but it's still one of the best!
