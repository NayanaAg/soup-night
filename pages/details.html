Not sure what soup to bring?

Worry not! I have prepared an extremely easy "quiz" for you to decide on a soup you can bring. Choose the image below that speaks to you the most, and I'll recommend you a soup based on its vibes.

Crunchy autumn leaves

Tomato soup

As someone who enjoys warm and comforting flavours, tomato soup is the perfect choice for you! Its simple but delicious flavours make it one of the most popular classic soups in the world. Great choice!

An elegant study

French onion soup

You enjoy the finer things in life, and this is true even for your soup choices. The complex, old-fashioned flavour of french onion soup suits you very well. Fun fact, this is also Wally's favourite soup, so you may end up competing with him to see who can make the frenchest and onionest soup.

An exciting roller coaster

Tom yum soup

You are someone who enjoys the adrenalin rush of extreme sports (like eating food spicy enough to make you cry), and the strong hot and sour flavours of tom yum soup feel exactly like being on a rollercoaster. This is my favourite soup so you've objectively picked the best option of the six, congratulations!
