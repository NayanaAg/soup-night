Here's my design process!

Even though the assignment was to make a mini site, I struggle to develop pages if I don't have a design plan in mind. That's why I spent some time before I started my github repo to make basic wireframes, figure out my colour palette, and brainstorm what elements would be included on the page. This let me prepare a lot of the mental work ahead of time, making the development process a lot smoother.

Lofi wireframes

For lofi wireframes, I always focus on content hierarchy and some of the unique design elements that I want to see repeated throughout the site (like the blockquotes in this case). Some things changed around in the design process of course, but I was happy to see that the main elements and style remained consistent between the lofi frames and the final developed product!

Hifi-ish wireframes

My main intention for hi-fi wireframes is to figure out what colours I'll be using, what fonts I need to import, and how many images / other media assets I'll need. As you can see, I didn't fill out the content fully, but I tried to plan around the main blocks so I would be prepared ahead of time for the final developed product.
