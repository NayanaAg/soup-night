Here's my design process!

Even though the assignment was to make a mini site, I struggle to develop pages if I don't have a design plan in mind. That's why I spent some time before I started my github repo to make basic wireframes, figure out my colour palette, and brainstorm what elements would be included on the page. This let me prepare a lot of the mental work ahead of time, making the development process a lot smoother.
