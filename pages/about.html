Hello! I'm Nayana, and I love goofing off with my friends :)

Me posing with a cheese house we built for a friend's birthday!

Welcome to my silly website! I'm a UX designer with experience in web design, professional writing, and marketing strategy. I spent a long time thinking about what kind of website I wanted and after a couple ideas that fizzled out in the wireframing stage, I settled on a website about events. I love organizing fun activities and holidays for my friends, from things like trivia and scavenger hunts to elaborate dinners and paint nights. I'm hoping to keep updating this site with information about our next event and changing up the colour palette to match the theme. Soon, I'll also add a "blog" section with our past events so we can all look fondly back on pictures of our favourite moments together. See my design process here.

For any feedback about this site or to discuss my work experiences, please fill out the contact form below. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you enjoyed this microsite!
